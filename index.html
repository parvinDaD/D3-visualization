Insight into Air Quality index relation with health

Just like how it is normal for people to check the weather condition forecasts to plan their outdoor activities, considering air pollution is also an important fact of daily life in industrial cities, and people should be mindful on how to avoid hazardous effects of air pollution. This visualization is aimed to give detailed information on proper activity level and sensitive age groups based on amount of contaminants in predicted Air Quality Index, thus people have a better comprehension on how to plan a polluted day.
