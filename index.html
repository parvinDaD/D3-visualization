Influential Women Of 21st Century

We take a look at some of the groundbreaking and inspirational females, who have been pioneers in leading fields of science and social good through 21st century. Each of these women dared to challeng convention and step outside of their expected roles to be aspiring for others in the society and younger generation. This visualization also demonstrates engagement of women in different fields. It is obseravable that there is still a great gap between participation of women in imperical versus applied sciences.
